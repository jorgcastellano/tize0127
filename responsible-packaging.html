    RESPONSIBLE PACKAGING

RICA has made the choice of minimizing the weight of plastic bottles and to maximizing the use of post-consumer recycled materials, using materials that can be and are recyclable. All of our bottles are made from Recycled PCR. Post Consumer Regrind (PCR) is made from reclaimed neutral PET. Other packaging is made from PET, PP or PETG, and can be easily recycled through local recycling programs. We use only Recycled Paper accredited by FSC.
