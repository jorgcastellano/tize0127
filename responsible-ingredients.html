AQ-SAVE™

AQ-SAVE is an active ingredient derived from organic fruit Castanea Sativa grown in Italy. The cosmetic activity is linked to fatty acids, proteins and to short chain sugars. Laboratory tests have shown that AQ-SAVE repairs the surface of the stressed hair with efficacy comparable to that of synthetic silicones. AQ-SAVE covers and repairs the hair by acting on it from the outside, this activity gives the hair a greater ability to retain water and consequently a greater hydration counteracting the hair stressing effects of thermal styling and pollution.
