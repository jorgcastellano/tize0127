RESPONSIBLE INGREDIENTS

A commitment to creating natural haircare products starts with a clear checklist on what haircare products should NOT contain.

OUR PRODUCTS ARE FREE OF:

  • Sodium Lauryl Sulphate (SLS)
  • Sodium Laureth Sulphate (SLES)
  • Parabens
  • Sulfates
  • Artificial dyes
  • Mineral oils, etc.

RICA PRODUCTS CONTAIN MANY NATURAL INGREDIENTS:

  • Volcanic Water
  • Opuntia Oil
  • Red Sicilian Orange
  • Capperis Spinosa
  • Wheat protein
  • Grape Extract
  • Rosemary
  • Pistachio Oil
  • Sage

  • AQ-SAVE™
